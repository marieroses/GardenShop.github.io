Who We Are

Based in Portarlington, Co. Laois, The Garden Shop was founded in 2011 with the principle of offering a wide range of garden products at the best value in Ireland. With our low-cost model and a strong focus on customer service, our online garden shop quickly expanded in the Irish market.We offer something for everyone—whether you’re a beginner starting out with a few small pots and trays on your windowsill, a ‘green-fingered’ expert, or a professional landscape gardener, we have the tools and supplies you need.

What We Do

The Garden Shop was created to fill a massive gap in the garden supplies and tools market. There are currently no other discount online garden centers in Ireland where people can get all their gardening equipment at heavily discounted prices. We aim to break the existing norms in garden pricing, giving the Irish gardening market a much-needed shake-up.We stock over 2,500 different products and are constantly searching out top-quality tools, unique plants, and handy garden equipment to offer at great low prices. Our selection includes respected gardening brands such as Felco, Spears & Jackson, Wolf Garden, Wilkinson Sword, Oleo Mac, and more.

How We Do It

We work tirelessly behind the scenes to maintain our high standards in customer service, shipping, and product quality. In May 2021, we expanded further by opening our eco garden shop and food village in Portarlington, called SOLAS. This eco-friendly space offers a wide range of environmentally friendly products, along with popular garden plants, seeds, tools, landscaping equipment, gardening accessories, furniture, and more.Our team is always ready to help, with experts available to provide advice and guidance.
